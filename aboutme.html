About Me

Abin Tamrakar




ARTIST / GRAPHIC DESIGNER / ANIMATOR

Hi, I'm an artist and a graphic designer based in Sydney, Australia. I'm originally from Nepal. Art became my passion from the day I saw my dad carving and designing Newari characters in sculptures. And since coming from a rich art and culture loving country, I fell in love with my passion even more . I was motivated seeing other's artworks in social media which led me to join Bara Fine Art Institute. Learning from my mistakes and dedicating my time in artistry, I made this my passion.
